Saudi Chicken Kabsa Recipe

Ingredients

For the Chicken & Rice:

  • 1 whole chicken (cut into 4 or 6 pieces)
  • 2 cups basmati rice (washed and soaked for 30 minutes)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 medium tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 large carrot (grated or thinly sliced)
  • 1/4 cup raisins (optional)
  • 4 cups water or chicken broth
  • Salt, to taste

Kabsa Spice Mix (or use store-bought if available):

  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
Instructions
  • Sauté Onions & Garlic: Heat oil in a large pot. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden. Add garlic and stir until fragrant.
  • Add Chicken & Spices: Add the chicken pieces and brown on all sides. Sprinkle the Kabsa spice mix and mix well.
  • Tomato Base: Add chopped tomatoes and tomato paste. Cook until tomatoes are soft and blended into a sauce.
  • Simmer: Add grated carrots and raisins. Pour in water or broth. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat and simmer (covered) for about 30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked.
  • Remove Chicken: Take out the chicken pieces and set aside. Optionally, bake or grill the chicken briefly for a crisp top.
  • Cook the Rice: Add soaked and drained rice to the pot. Stir gently. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and cook on low until rice is done (about 15–20 minutes).
  • Serve: Place rice on a large serving platter. Arrange the chicken on top. Garnish with nuts and parsley if desired.
