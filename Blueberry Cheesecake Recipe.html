Blueberry Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients:

For the Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs (or digestive biscuits)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter (melted)

For the Cheesecake Filling:

  • 3 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened (680g)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sour cream or heavy cream

For the Blueberry Topping:

  • 2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons water
Instructions:

1- Prepare the Crust:

  • Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C).
  • Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter in a bowl.
  • Press mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  • Bake for 10 minutes. Remove and let cool.

    • 2- Make the Cheesecake Filling:

  • In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth.
  • Add sugar and mix until well blended.
  • Beat in eggs one at a time, then add vanilla and sour cream.
  • Pour the filling over the cooled crust.

    • 3- Bake the Cheesecake:

  • Place the pan in a larger roasting pan filled with hot water (optional water bath for a smooth top).
  • Bake for 50–60 minutes until center is almost set.
  • Turn off oven, crack the oven door, and let the cheesecake cool inside for 1 hour.
  • Then refrigerate for at least 4 hours (overnight is best).

    • 4- Make the Blueberry Topping:

  • In a small saucepan, combine blueberries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and water.
  • Cook over medium heat until it thickens and becomes syrupy (about 5–7 minutes).
  • Let it cool completely.

    • 5- Serve:

  • Spoon the blueberry topping over the chilled cheesecake.
  • Slice and enjoy!
