Creamy Alfredo Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250g fettuccine pasta (or your favorite kind)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning or nutmeg
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

    • Optional Add-ins:

  • Grilled chicken or shrimp
  • Mushrooms or spinach

Instructions:

1- Cook the Pasta:

  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  • Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions.
  • Drain and set aside (reserve a bit of pasta water for later).

2- Make the Alfredo Sauce:

  • In a large pan over medium heat, melt the butter.
  • Add minced garlic and sauté for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  • Pour in the heavy cream and stir. Simmer for 3–5 minutes (don’t boil).
  • Pour in the heavy cream and stir. Simmer for 3–5 minutes (don’t boil).

3- Combine:

  • Add the cooked pasta to the sauce.
  • Toss to coat evenly. If it feels too thick, add a splash of reserved pasta water.
  • Season with salt, pepper, and optional Italian seasoning or nutmeg.
